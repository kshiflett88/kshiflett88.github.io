Fitness 4 Hope

Inpired by the wonderful children from Our Learning House

Fitness 4 Hope is an international community based in Beijing, China. Founded by Pablo De Arriba Fernandez and myself. We firmly believe that every child deserves the opportunity to grow up healthy and happy. Combining our love for volunteering and fitness, F4H was born.

How can fitness events help children in need?

The unifying power of sports naturally brings people together. With that we built an international community of like-minded people by hosting free fitness classes for children and adults; boxing, HIIT, zumba and yoga. At the end of each class we set time to talk about our mission to help the children at Our Learning House (OLH). Attendees could then donate any amount directly to the orphanage and/or sign up to volunteer at OLH.

This correlates with our four core values of Giving, Health, Empowerment and Cultural Awareness. Giving our time to others, instilling healthier habits, empowering others through teamwork, pushing past limits and bringing a variety of cultures together in a safe and positive environment.

Around the world

F4H has not just stayed in China. We have also partnered up with Travel Teachers in India. Giving us the opportunity to visit various schools in the south of India. Inauguarating sport facilities for elementary and middle schools, speaking on a panel at universities and high schools and instructing fitness classes to teachers and students. We have raised money by running a marathon in Japan and started a video challenge of parents exercising with their children and received videos from over 6 countries.
