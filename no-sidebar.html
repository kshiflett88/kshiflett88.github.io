Mt. Fuji

I took this picture while hiking in the Yamanashi prefecture. The sacred and iconic symbol of Japan. Standing 12,388ft (3,777 metres) it is the highest peak in Japan and can be seen from several surrounding prefectures.

The Great Wall of China

This photo is taken by me at Huanghuacheng "Yellow Flower City" and is the only lakeside Great Wall in Beijing. One of 7 the world wonders The Great Wall is a symbol of enduring strength. There are many sections of the great wall that are not preserved and are thus known as the "Wild Wall."

Kolukkumalai

I took this picture during my trip to Kerala, India. At an altitude of 7,900 feet, Kolukkumalai tea plantations are the highest in the world. This high altitude gives the tea a special flavor and freshness you wont find anywhere else in the world.
